Creative Subject #6 (CS#6). Traffic Counts

We chose from the Netherlands AIP two adjacent sectors. We gathered the data on the coordinates to plot and separate the area targeted by the rest of the map (fact observed through the outline of the sectors).

Then, the main airports of the globe were chosen and random routes were created to see which of them transit through the chosen area. Once an aircraft transits the flight sectors concerned, the counting function takes it into account and adds it to the register. Those that do not pass the space, do not affect anything.

*This application helps predict the transit on a sector. Thus, it is possible to estimate the number of flights that will transit certain areas for the opening of the required sectors. By doing this, the problem of capacity can be avoided.
